A Little About Me


Frequently Asked Questions

  • Where are you from?

    I was born and raised right in the greater Hudson Valley area, about an hour north of New York City in Putnam County, NY. I grew up in a medium to small sized town named Brewster, NY.

  • Where and what did you study?

    I started off learning and graduating from Brewster High School in early 2017, this was just the jumping off point of the career. Later on, I got my Bachelors of Science degree in Computer Science at the State University of New York at New Paltz (SUNY New Paltz) in May of 2021. Today, I focus on self taught web development as well as securing certifications for future IT endeavors.

  • Why computer science?

    I have always had a knack for mathematics, and the logic behind the numbers. I loved how I could incorporate logic and math into a bigger picture. Computer Science, and especially web development, have allowed me to apply myself and my skills creatively, not only to solve problems, but to create software products that people can enjoy. Software development is more than just numbers and algorithms.

  • What about your free time?

    I enjoy many different activities besides software development. Contrary to coding, I love to get out in nature and go on hikes when I am able. Going to see new trails with friends or venturing out for some urban exploration is always thrilling. When I'm at home, I enjoy learning new songs on my guitar or settling in for some online gaming with friends.
